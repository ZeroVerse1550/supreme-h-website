ABOUT

Supreme H is a South African deep house DJ and music producer known for crafting emotionally charged, groove-driven music that moves both the body and the soul. Based in Tsakane, East Rand, Supreme H represents a new generation of deep house artists delivering authentic sound, modern energy, and memorable live experiences.

Born Lehlonolo, Supreme H began his professional music journey in 2021 and quickly established himself as a focused and disciplined artist with a clear sonic identity. His sound is rooted in deep and soulful house, enriched with afro-influenced rhythms and warm melodic elements—creating sets that are immersive, uplifting, and perfectly tuned to the dance floor.

Supreme H has performed at respected venues such as Chillas Lifestyle and The Smoked House, and previously held a resident DJ position at Welcome Place, where he refined his crowd-reading ability and performance control. Known for smooth transitions and intentional track selection, he consistently delivers sets that build energy, connect with the audience, and leave a lasting impression.

As a producer, Supreme H continues to grow a strong and consistent discography. His recent 2025 releases highlight both his artistic maturity and his readiness for bigger stages. Tracks like "Euphoria" showcase peak-time energy and club-ready emotion, while "Far Away" explores a deeper, more soulful expression—demonstrating his versatility and range.
